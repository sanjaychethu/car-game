    ABOUT CODEFURY 7.0

    CodeFury is IEEE UVCE Computer Society's Annual National Level Hackathon. Our vision is to conduct Hackathons every year to help solve some of the major problems faced by society and the industry, thus ensuring a massive impact on the world.

    Participants can sit in the comfort of their home and develop solutions to real-world problems by developing either a Web Application or an Android Application within 24 hours. Brace yourselves for one of the best events you've experienced so far. Mark your calendars on 23rd and 24th of July 2024 as we will be conducting CodeFury 7.0 on these days.Get a chance to compete against peers across the country and receive certificates and cash prizes too!

    IEEE UVCE Computer Society is one of the most prominent societies that comes under IEEE. It acts as a great platform for students of all branches to learn more and contribute to the computing world. IEEE UVCE Computer Society has been conducting many events since its inception which include the Annual National Level Hackathon CodeFury, the monthly coding competition NCode and several workshops to kindle students' interest, to update their knowledge on new upcoming technological advances and improve their programming skills as well.

    WEB-DEV-TRACK

    Are you a coding virtuoso with an eye for captivating web designs and seamless user experiences? Look no further! The web development track at CodeFury 7.0 is the perfect arena for you to showcase your web development skills, collaborate with fellow developers, and compete for the coveted cash prize.

    In this exhilarating 24-hour hackathon, you'll have the opportunity to dive into the world of web development. From front-end wizardry to back-end mastery, the possibilities are limitless. Unleash your coding creativity and build captivating websites that leave a lasting impression.

    Within the intense 24-hour time frame, participants will be tasked with designing, coding, and presenting their remarkable web projects to a panel of esteemed judges. This is your chance to demonstrate your problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and innovation in web development.

    As a token of appreciation for your hard work and dedication, CodeFury 7.0 offers an incredible cash prize for the winners of the web development track. The top-performing teams will be rewarded with a share of the 20,000 prize pool, along with well-deserved recognition for their exceptional web development skills.

    The web development track is open to both seasoned web developers and aspiring enthusiasts. Whether you're a coding veteran or an eager newcomer, CodeFury 7.0 welcomes participants of all levels of expertise.

    FAQs

    WHAT IS CODEFURY AND WHEN IS IT?

    Codefury is a 24 hours Annual National-Level Hackathon conducted by IEEE UVCE Computer Society.

    WHY SHOULD I PARTICIPATE IN CODEFURY 7.0?

    You stand a chance to get cash prizes if you win; if not, you will always get an excellent opportunity to network with like-minded people and learn many things.

    ARE THE PARTICIPANTS REQUIRED TO USE A PARTICULAR TECH STACK?

    No, participants may use any tech stack of their choice to develop a web or a mobile application.

    WHAT KIND OF PROBLEM STATEMENTS ARE EXPECTED?

    It can be anything. Here are the problem statements from last year-
    1) An application for women safety
    2) An application to assist the agriculture sector
    3) An application to provide jobs for unorganized sector post covid.
    4) An application which provides a virtual learning environment where like minded individuals can present their resources,activities and interact within a course structure.

    HOW WILL THE TEAMS BE JUDGED?

    The teams will be judged based on Technical Complexity, Presentation Skills and most importantly, creativity.

    DO I NEED PRIOR EXPERIENCE IN CODING?

    Basic programming knowledge is all you need to participate; experience is not required for joining this hackathon.
